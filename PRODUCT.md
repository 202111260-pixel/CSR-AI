# Product

## Register

product

## Users

CSR officers, sustainability managers, and executives at Omani corporations. They work in office environments — often checking the dashboard before board meetings or during incidents. On any given session they are: triaging active alerts, identifying which projects need intervention, reviewing AI-generated recommendations, and approving or rejecting remediation scenarios. The primary task is "what needs my attention right now, and what do I do about it."

## Product Purpose

An Omani Corporate Social Responsibility management platform. Tracks CSR project lifecycle (planning → active → on_hold → completed → archived), budgets, beneficiaries, impact reports, partner donations, employee ideas, and early-warning alerts across Oman's 11 governorates. Success = managers can monitor and act on risk signals without opening a single project detail page.

## Brand Personality

Authoritative, precise, civic. The platform serves real government and corporate decisions — it should feel like a trusted instrument, not a startup app. Tone: serious but not cold, confident but not flashy.

## Anti-references

- Generic SaaS dashboards (Linear-white, Vercel-minimal, Notion-neutral)
- Dark-mode clichés: neon-on-black crypto/fintech aesthetic
- Alert-red disaster overload — every element screaming urgency
- Corporate navy + gold played straight (it's the platform's existing palette, not a destination)
- Bootstrap or Material Design table-heavy admin panels

## Design Principles

1. **Urgency is earned** — only critical signals should pulse or glow; medium/low should be calm and readable
2. **Scannable in 30 seconds** — the most important information (what is critical, what needs action) must be visible without scrolling
3. **Data over decoration** — visual effects must carry information, not just look impressive
4. **Action-forward** — every alert should have a clear next step visible without expanding
5. **Platform coherence** — redesigns on individual pages should feel like an evolution of the system, not a theme swap

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA minimum. User has low vision — text minimum 12px, no color-only encoding, sufficient contrast on all interactive elements. Arabic RTL support required (platform is bilingual EN/AR).
