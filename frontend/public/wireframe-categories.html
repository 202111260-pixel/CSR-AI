Description
The Education category encompasses all CSR initiatives focused on improving access to quality education across Oman's governorates. Projects include building and renovating schools, providing scholarships, digital literacy programs, vocational training centers, and supporting university partnerships. Aligned with Vision 2040 and SDG Goal 4, this category prioritizes sustainable learning outcomes and community capacity building.
